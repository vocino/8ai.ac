DiffusionBee is a macOS application that makes it easy to run Stable Diffusion and Flux locally without technical knowledge or command-line usage. Download the app, enter a text prompt, and generate images—all processing happens on your Mac with no data sent to the cloud. DiffusionBee supports Apple Silicon (M1 and later), offers generative fill, image upscaling, and custom model training, and is ideal for Mac users who want simple, private AI art generation.
