Udio is an AI music generation platform developed by former Google DeepMind researchers. It generates high-quality, fully produced music tracks from text descriptions and supports a broad range of genres. Distinctive features include inpainting (editing specific segments of a track) and stems export for professional post-production. Udio is known for particularly strong vocal and harmonic quality.
