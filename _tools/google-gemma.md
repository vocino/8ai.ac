Google Gemma is a family of lightweight, open-weight AI models built from the same research and technology as Google's Gemini models. Available in sizes from 2B to 27B parameters, Gemma models are optimized for on-device deployment, local inference, and fine-tuning. They deliver strong performance relative to their size and are freely available for download from Hugging Face, Kaggle, and Google AI Studio.
