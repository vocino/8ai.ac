The Classify Agent Pixel is a free, lightweight JavaScript snippet that publishers embed to detect when AI agents are crawling or browsing their content. As AI agents increasingly drive referral and research traffic, most publishers have no tooling to measure this segment. The pixel fills that gap with a single `<script>` tag.

## How It Works

1. Embed the pixel on any web property
2. The pixel fingerprints inbound traffic against a database of known AI agent signatures
3. Detected agent visits surface alongside a revenue estimate based on your traffic and content vertical

## Revenue Calculator

The companion calculator at [tryclassify.com/agent-traffic-revenue-calculator](https://tryclassify.com/agent-traffic-revenue-calculator) lets publishers model potential revenue from agent monetization before committing to any paid product.

## Who It's For

- Publishers and media companies quantifying AI traffic exposure
- SSPs and ad tech platforms evaluating agentic inventory
- Developers building on the agentic web who need traffic attribution data
