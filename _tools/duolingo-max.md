Duolingo Max is the premium tier of Duolingo that adds AI-powered features to the popular language learning app. It includes Roleplay, which lets learners practice conversations with AI characters in real-world scenarios, and Explain My Answer, which provides personalized grammar and vocabulary explanations. The Video Call feature offers immersive speaking practice with AI tutors. These features use GPT-4 to provide natural, adaptive language learning experiences.
