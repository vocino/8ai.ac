Duolingo Max is the highest subscription tier of Duolingo that adds advanced AI-powered learning features on top of the core app. Learners can practice natural conversations with characters, get detailed explanations of why answers are right or wrong, and use richer interactive exercises. It is designed to close gaps between app-based learning and real-world communication by giving more feedback and realistic practice opportunities.
