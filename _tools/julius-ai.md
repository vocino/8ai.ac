Julius AI is an AI data analyst that lets anyone explore and visualize data through natural language conversation. Users upload spreadsheets, databases, or connect to Google Sheets, then ask questions like "show me monthly revenue trends" or "which product has the highest return rate." Julius writes and executes Python or R code behind the scenes and returns charts, statistics, and written insights.
