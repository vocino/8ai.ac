Whisper is OpenAI's open-source automatic speech recognition (ASR) system trained on 680,000 hours of multilingual audio data. It delivers robust transcription across 100 languages and can translate speech into English. Whisper is available both as an open-source model for local deployment and through the OpenAI API. It has become the foundation for countless transcription tools and voice applications.
