Consensus is an AI-powered search engine designed to answer questions using evidence from peer-reviewed scientific research. Its unique Consensus Meter aggregates findings across multiple studies to show what percentage of research supports a claim. It is popular with medical professionals, students, journalists, and policy makers who need quick access to evidence-based information.
