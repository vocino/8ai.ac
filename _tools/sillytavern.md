SillyTavern is a frontend for connecting to local LLMs (e.g., via Kobold, Oobabooga) or remote APIs. It supports character cards, roleplay scenarios, and extensions for image gen and TTS. SillyTavern runs locally and gives users full control over their AI interactions.
