Meta Llama is Meta's family of open-source large language models, widely regarded as the most capable openly available LLMs. The Llama 3 family includes models from 8B to 405B parameters, with multimodal variants supporting vision. Models are downloadable from Meta's website and Hugging Face, and can be run locally, fine-tuned, or deployed via third-party inference providers.
