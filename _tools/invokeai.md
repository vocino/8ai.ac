InvokeAI is a free and open-source creative engine for AI-powered image generation. Built by creatives for creatives, it offers a powerful WebUI, advanced node-based workflows, and layer-based canvas editing for full control over the generative process. Invoke runs entirely on your own hardware, supports Stable Diffusion and SDXL, and is Apache 2.0 licensed. The project is community-maintained with active development and comprehensive documentation.
