Galileo AI generates high-fidelity UI designs from text prompts or reference images. Describe a fintech dashboard, landing page, or app screen, and it produces polished mockups in seconds. The tool is trained on UI patterns and conventions for realistic outputs. Designers use it for rapid exploration, client pitches, and learning established patterns. Generated designs export to Figma or as HTML/Tailwind code. Galileo offers a free tier and paid plans for teams.
