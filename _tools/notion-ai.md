Notion AI is the embedded AI layer in Notion, the popular productivity and note-taking platform. It allows users to summarize long documents, draft new content, translate pages into other languages, extract action items from meeting notes, and ask questions that search across their entire Notion workspace. Notion AI is available as an add-on for all plan tiers.
