Khanmigo is Khan Academy's AI teaching assistant and tutor. Unlike generic chatbots, it uses Socratic dialogue to guide learners to discover answers through critical thinking. Khanmigo supports teachers with lesson planning, rubrics, and progress reports at no cost. For learners and parents, it offers on-demand tutoring, writing help, and homework assistance for $4/month. Powered by GPT-4, it emphasizes safety with parental controls and content moderation. Rated highly by Common Sense Media.
