Lindy is a platform for creating AI agents that handle repetitive work across sales, marketing, operations, support, and other functions. Teams can assemble workflows visually or via API, connect them to their existing tools, and let agents run tasks with monitoring and approvals where needed. It targets companies that want automation and AI assistance tailored to their own data and processes rather than generic chatbots.
