Photomath is an AI-powered math learning app that lets users scan math problems with their camera and receive instant step-by-step solutions. It covers topics from basic arithmetic through calculus and provides multiple solving methods with detailed explanations. Acquired by Google in 2022, Photomath has become one of the most popular educational AI tools with over 300 million downloads worldwide.
