Jasper (formerly Jarvis) is an AI writing assistant purpose-built for marketing teams. It uses a Brand Voice feature to learn a company's tone, terminology, and style guidelines, ensuring all AI-generated content stays on-brand. Jasper supports long-form blog posts, ad copy, email campaigns, landing pages, and social media content, and integrates with SEO tools for optimized output.
