Qwen is Alibaba Cloud's open-weight large language model family, offering models ranging from 0.5B to 110B parameters. The Qwen2.5 series delivers competitive performance across text generation, coding, mathematics, and reasoning tasks. The family includes specialized variants for vision (Qwen-VL), audio (Qwen-Audio), and code (Qwen-Coder). Qwen models are freely available for download and self-hosting, making them a popular choice for developers building AI applications globally.
