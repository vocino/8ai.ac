Mistral AI is a French AI company founded in 2023 that develops both open-weight and proprietary large language models. Their open-source models (Mistral 7B, Mixtral 8x7B) are available on Hugging Face for self-hosting, while commercial models like Mistral Large are accessible via their API platform "La Plateforme." They also offer Le Chat, a consumer chatbot.
